Green Initiatives

 

 A significant portion of the Craftsbury Outdoor Center's mission and activities relate to sustainability and the environment. The mission involves the goals of using and teaching sustainable practices and protecting and managing the surrounding land, lake and trails. Projects at the Center always strive to include the latest advances in green technology and adapt to best serve the land around us. These projects can loosely broken in to four categories: Buildings and Energy Use, Waste Management, Local Food Production and Procurement, and Land and Lake.
