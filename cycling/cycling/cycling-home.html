Cycling Home

Craftsbury's involvement with bikes goes back into the 80s - the roots of the sport. Since that time, we've added a bunch of trails, with much improved siting and equipment. We host races every Tuesday, plus bigger events during the summer and fall. We have programs for kids or school groups to come learn to ride. We can rent you a bike, or just help you find your next favorite ride. Join us for the day, or base yourself here. Hope you'll come up and visit!
