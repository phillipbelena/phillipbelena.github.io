Nordic Center Home

We groom 105k of trails connecting Northeast Kingdom villages, farms, field and forest most days from November thru March. Enjoy our trails as a day skier, or come up for an extended stay in our trailside lodging; we have rentals, lessons, and amazing food to fuel whatever adventure you have in mind. Check our snow report and get planning!
