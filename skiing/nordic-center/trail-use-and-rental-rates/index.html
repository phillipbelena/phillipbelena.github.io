Lessons

Beginner or expert, our ski school can accommodate your needs. Whether you are race training or standing on skis for the first time, our experienced friendly staff will custom design a classical or skate skiing lesson for you. We specialize in making learning to ski fun for both kids and adults.

Private Lesson for one person: $45
Private Lesson for two people: $60
Private Lesson for three to eight: $75 (with $10 per each additional person)

While we can often accommodate walk-ins, reserving your lesson time is recommended. Reach out to the ski shop:  802-586-7767 x303

Shuttles

As soon as conditions are good during the season, we run a shuttle bus Saturdays and Sundays to the Craftsbury-groomed and maintained Greensboro Trails. We ask a fee of $5 for the shuttle to cover the cost of bus use, pay at the Activity Center prior to boarding (Bus departs 9:30am). Max capacity of 48 passengers.

While we plan to run the shuttle both weekend days, in the event of poor weather or trail conditions, we will not run the shuttle – call ahead to confirm! Ski Shop staff will make the decision about shuttle running at 4:30pm the day before. 

When will this get started? We plan to start in January 2019, but this service is entirely weather dependent. Even when scheduled, it's always worth a call to the Touring Center to confirm the current shuttle schedule and availability.

Shuttle runs from the Center's Touring Center to Highland Lodge where you can access Greensboro Trails.

Weekend shuttle
Leaves Craftsbury: 9:30am
Arrives Greensboro: 9:45am

The above schedule will run January - February. We determine spring schedules based on demand - good to give a confirming call before making the drive up!

Biathlon Lessons

Biathlon lessons are available for interested individuals ages 10 and up. Lessons are about one hour long and can be designed for all ability levels, from those who have never shot before to those who are familiar with the sport and want to hone their skills. Lessons include an intro to the sport of biathlon, rifle safety, and live fire shooting. More info about biathlon at Craftsbury here.

Private Lesson for one person: $45
Private Lesson for two people: $60
Private Lesson for three to eight: $75 (with $10 per each additional person) 

Book a lesson by talking to the Touring Center, calling the office at 802.586.7767, or emailing biathlon@craftsbury.com.

Ski Trail Use Guidelines

We are grateful to the many landowners who allow us to groom trails on their property. Please respect the land we ski on.

  • Unless signed as one-way, all trails are two-way trails. Always be aware of oncoming skiers.
  • Stay to the right when meeting oncoming skiers.
  • Downhill skiers have the right of way.
  • If you stop, please step to the side of the trail.
  • Please do not damage the classic track by skating or herringboning on top of it.
  • Skiing on closed trails is prohibited and potentially dangerous.
  • Dogs are welcome on the lakes. Check at Nordic Center for other options.
  • Some of our trails intersect and join snowmobile trails, use caution in these areas. Please be aware of the shared use, and note where the ski trail departs again. 
  • Fatbikes share selected ski trails, under certain conditions. Cyclists, review those rules before heading out.
  • All biathlon range users must have trail pass/membership and prior approval to shoot from COC biathlon staff. Please contact biathlon@craftsbury.com to schedule range use at least 24 hours in advance.
