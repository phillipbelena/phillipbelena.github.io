Registration:

We highly recommend that you pre-register online via Webscorer. It's easy, doesn't cost anything, saves tons of typing and time, significantly streamlines bib pickup, and ensures that your name will be spelled correctly and your series points will be scored properly.

More reasons to pre-reg? You'll also get results emailed to you and updates about upcoming races straight to your inbox. If you think you may race more than once, we encourage registering for the entire series - there's no harm if you miss a race, and it makes the whole process substantially easier, for you and for us!

Online registration is available here for the whole series.

Day-of registration will still be available. All participants, whether pre-reg or day-of, will need to check-in and pick up their bibs in the Activity Center. Check-in opens at 3:15 PM and closes at 3:45 PM. Races will start in the lower field at 4:00 PM. 

The race is free if you're a Craftsbury member or houseguest, otherwise you will need a day membership to cover your entry. 

Series Scoring: 

We will calculate series standings and offer season-long prizes for both overall and age group results, based on points from each event. Full details on the scoring system can be found here. You can also keep an eye on series standings over at Webscorer, once the series gets going.

Updates and Announcements:

Info, including changes to the schedule, results, and updated course maps will be posted as news items on our front page, linked here and on social media, and emailed to all Webscorer registrants. Email any questions to tnr@craftsbury.com.

Timing:

We need volunteers, every race week! Volunteers will help with registration, timing, and bib collecting. Volunteering counts toward volunteer hours (e.g. JR Scholarships) and enters you for end-of-season volunteer prizes. Be a good ski citizen, support the community, and volunteer via tnr@craftsbury.com. 

Temperature:

We won't race if the temp is 0F (-17C) or below. Watch the website, social media, and your inbox for announcements.
