Sculling

Sculling Program Group Master List
Craftsbury offers a 10% discount for groups for non-peak sculling camp weeks. Six or more campers from the same school or rowing club constitute a group within our sculling camp. All group members must attend the same camp session. Groups are encouraged to apply early as all reservations (including groups) are accepted on the basis of availability of space. The discount will be applied to each individual's reservation when the group arrives at camp - discount will not be applied retroactively to groups formed at camp.

To qualify for a group discount, the following needs to be submitted at the time of reservation:

  1. A Sculling Program Group Master List MUST BE SUBMITTED PRIOR TO YOUR ARRIVAL. Download PDF form.
  2. A registration form for each individual in the group. Online form.
  3. A group deposit equal to $250 x number of group members, each member may use their own check or credit card on their individual registration form.

Any questions can be directed to the Craftsbury Sculling Center office at 802-586-7767, or by email at stay@craftsbury.com.

Running

Craftsbury offers a 10% discount for groups for camps. Four or more campers from the same school or USATF running club constitute a group within our running camp. All group members must attend the same camp session. Groups are encouraged to apply early as all reservations (including groups) are accepted on the basis of availability of space. The discount will be applied to each individual's reservation when the group arrives at camp - discount will not be applied retroactively to groups formed at camp.

To qualify for a group discount, the following needs to be submitted at the time of reservation:

  1. A Running Program Group Master List MUST BE SUBMITTED PRIOR TO YOUR ARRIVAL. Download PDF form.
  2. A registration form for each individual in the group. Online form.
  3. A group deposit equal to $250 x number of group members, each member may use their own check or credit card on their individual registration form.
