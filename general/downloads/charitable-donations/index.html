Charitable Donations

The Craftsbury Outdoor Center is now a non-profit entity. As such, our cash donations to other non-profits will be limited. However, we will still consider in-kind donations to non-profit organizations that are local and/or closely aligned with our mission. In kind items may include such things as meals, lessons, & memberships.

If you're interested in a donation from the Center, the first step is to complete a copy of the donation request form below. The management team makes efforts to review the requests regularly and contact you as soon as possible there after so that you can do your planning and so forth at your end as well.
