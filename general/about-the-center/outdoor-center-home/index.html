Groups and Lodging Home

You may have heard of us as the first rowing camp in North America, or perhaps as the most consistent snow in the east, but even without participating in one of our programs, you can share the same great Craftsbury setting, excellent food and facilities. We've hosted professional groups seeking natural beauty and tranquility, family reunions large and small, and families and individuals who just want to enjoy our unrivaled grounds and comfortable lodging. And now we've reorganized as a non-profit to ensure that the Craftsbury experience will be around for generations to come. Read our mission or find more information on lodging, dining and activities.
