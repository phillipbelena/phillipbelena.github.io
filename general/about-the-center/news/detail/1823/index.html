Girls with Guns Biathlon Clinic

Last Updated: 22.Feb.2017

On Sunday, March 26, the Craftsbury Outdoor Center will be hosting the first ever Girls with Guns biathlon clinic. The event will feature World Championship silver medalist Susan Dunklee and three-time national team member Clare Egan (both Green Racing Project athletes). Activities will include rifle safety, learning how to shoot a biathlon rifle, skiing, and hanging out with national team biathletes. Come prepared to ski and be outside rain or shine. Ski rentals are available in the touring center if needed. The clinic will be from 3:00-5:00pm, with day-of-registration beginning at 2:30pm. Girls aged 8-18, of all abilities, are encouraged to attend - whether you know how to ski and shoot or not! Stick around after the Spring Fling for this exciting opportunity to try biathlon!

Preregistration is available at Skireg. Questions? Email Emily.
