Lodging Rates

Jump to Seasonal Lodging Rates for: Summer | Off-season | Winter

Craftsbury is known for a lot of different things, from skiing to sculling, introductory experiences to high-level training. Perhaps the best way to immerse yourself in the Craftsbury experience is to stay on campus in our on-site lodging.

We offer a range of accommodations, from dorm-style with a shared bathroom and twins beds; up to apartment style suites and private lakeside accommodations. All accommodations are simple and comfortable and include three all-you-can-eat meals from our Vermont Fresh Network certified kitchen. A vegetarian option is offered at every meal, and with prior notice the kitchen can accommodate a variety of dietary needs..
