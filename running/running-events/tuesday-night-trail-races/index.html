Julbo Tuesday Night Trail Races

Series Reg | Points Scoring | 2019 Season Standings 

Looking for a low-key way to test your summer fitness? Starting on May 28th, our weekly trail series offers a 5k run and 5 or 10k bike race every Tuesday, for only $5/race or $30 for the entire series - annual members and houseguests race for free!

As usual, races will rotate between two courses at the Outdoor Center (see below) with a few trips up to Hosmer Point. Bikers and runners will alternate courses at the Center, with bikers contesting one course and runners the other. Course A is marked in yellows signs with blue arrows and Course B is marked in white signs with red arrows. In addition to the races at the Center and Hosmer Point, we're excited to return to the Hardwick Trails.

Bikers start at 5:45pm and runners start at 6:00pm. We will once again be offering online preregistration on Webscorer (which will be up-and-running soon) as well as day-of registration which opens at 5:15pm in the Activity Center/Touring Center. Pre-registrants will be entered to win a cookie prize every week! Day-of registration closes at 5:40pm for bikers and 5:50pm for runners. Even if you register online, please check in with the race organizers to collect your bib! And if you think you'll do more than one race, we encourage you to register for the whole series as it makes the whole process easier. The start line is on the Upper Soccer Field near the white tent.

Season long prizes for ages group winners and overall winners (see scoring rules) will be awarded at the end of season wood-fired pizza party. We're thrilled that Julbo Mountain Performance Eyewear is sponsoring the series for the third year in a row and will be providing the prizes for the overall winners. We hope to see folks of all ages participating in these fun races!
