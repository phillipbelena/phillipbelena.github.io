Running Home

For more than 40 years, runners of every level and all ages have come to Craftsbury for expert coaching, camaraderie, healthy food and an unmatched natural environment. Since being reorganized as a non-profit in the fall of 2008, the Center's mission has grown. Read more about the mission or go straight to our camp information.
